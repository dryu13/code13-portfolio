core features
Appointment Calendar

Day, week, and month views. Create, edit, cancel bookings. Recurring blocked times, waitlist, combo-services, and promo codes with discounts.

Client Management

Full client cards with visit history, files, notes, loyalty points, memberships, consent forms, birthday, gender, and SMS opt-in.

Payments

Zelle, cash, card, Stripe, Groupon, membership, and other. Stripe Checkout subtly encourages tipping — clients choose 10, 15, 18, or 20% at checkout, increasing average transaction value. Transparent 3% processing fee breakdown included.

Consent Form Builder

Custom form constructor with sections and any question type. QR code delivery — client fills on their phone, signed form saves to their card. PDF export.

Multi-Channel Notifications

Email via Resend, SMS via Twilio, Telegram bot — all from a single trigger. Booking confirmation, cancellation, 24h reminder. Incoming SMS forwarded to Telegram.

Online Booking

Public booking form at book.labaventura.com. Categories, combo-services, promo codes, SMS opt-in — clients book 24/7 without calling.

Google Calendar Sync

Two-way sync with Google Calendar. New bookings appear automatically; cancelled bookings are deleted. Always up to date on any device.

Retail Products

Product cards with photos, cost price and retail price. Record sales with discounts, generate payment links, track sales statistics — all inside the CRM.

Dashboard

Daily revenue summary, today's appointments, key metrics. See the full picture of the business at a glance every morning.

what makes it different
Consent Forms
Custom form builder with QR delivery

Build consent forms with any sections and question types. Client scans a QR code, fills the form on their phone — signed response saves directly to their card. PDF export included. Not found in standard beauty CRMs.

Notifications
Email + SMS + Telegram from one trigger

One booking event fires three parallel channels: email via Resend, SMS via Twilio, and a Telegram bot alert for staff. Incoming client SMS messages are also forwarded to Telegram — the team stays in one chat.

Payments
Transparent Stripe fee breakdown

Stripe Checkout shows the service price and the 3% processing fee as separate line items — clients see exactly what they're paying for. Tip options (10/15/18/20%) built in.

Calendar Sync
True two-way Google Calendar sync

Every booking syncs to Google Calendar automatically. When a booking is cancelled, it's deleted from Google Calendar too. Staff can view the schedule from any device without opening the CRM.

Products
Retail tracking built into the CRM

Product cards with cost price and retail price. Record sales with discounts, generate Stripe payment links, see sales statistics — retail and services tracked in one place.
